SpotIT LK

A Civic Engagement Platform

Tech Stack: Flutter | Dart | Firebase

Have you ever seen a massive pothole on your street, or a pile of uncollected garbage in your neighborhood, and wished there was an easy way to get it fixed? That is exactly why we built SpotIT LK. Right now, when people see a problem in their city, they usually just complain about it on Facebook or WhatsApp. The problem is, those posts rarely reach the right government officials, and they lack details like exact location coordinates. SpotIT LK changes that by turning your smartphone into a powerful civic tool.
